# What FRAME Lab Is

FRAME Lab is the testing ground for FRAME.

## Tiny idea

Think of FRAME like a machine we are building.
Frame Lab is the workshop where we keep testing the machine on real material.

If the machine jams, bends, or breaks, we do not clap for the blueprint.
We fix the machine.

## What the lab actually does

The lab takes a candidate FRAME shape and hits it against real repos.
Then it studies the dents.

That means:
- fitting the candidate to real projects
- scoring where it works or feels forced
- investigating weak claims
- adjusting the candidate only when pressure keeps repeating
- rerunning to see whether the change helped

## What the lab is not

It is not:
- a place to generate YAML for its own sake
- a place to defend an old schema because it already exists
- a place to make FRAME look bigger than it is

## Why this matters

Without the lab, FRAME could easily become:
- too abstract
- too Haxaml-shaped
- too bloated
- too tied to one repo family

The lab keeps the contract honest.
