# What Fitting Means

Fitting means taking a real repo and asking:

How would the current FRAME line describe this project without getting weird, bloated, or fake-useful?

## What a fit pass does

A fit pass usually tries to produce:
- `facts.yaml`
- `rules.yaml`
- `map.yaml`
- `expect.yaml`
- `acts.yaml`

Then it explains where the fit felt:
- natural
- forced
- incomplete
- repetitive

## What a good fit looks like

A good fit:
- captures the repo clearly
- uses simple names
- avoids repeated truth
- leaves weak or unknown things explicit
- keeps Acts honest about what was really done

## What a bad fit looks like

A bad fit:
- copies the same truth across files
- adds fields just to feel complete
- invents fake precision
- makes Acts look stronger than it really is
